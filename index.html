Welcome to the Cat Witch Mission


Our cat witch, as a loyal companion, needs your help in order to save its witch friend which was taken captive by the evil witch.

Your Cat

How to Play

  • Move the cat witch by using the arrow keys.
  • Collect the potions and the spell books for 10 points.
  • Avoid the evil spells shots from the evil witch.
  • If you hit the evil spells 9 times: GAME OVER! (Why 9? Cause you are helping a cat and why not?!)
  • WARNING: Avoid also the evil witch touching you, otherwise you lose your lives faster.
  • If you want to play again, just simply click on the replay button.
