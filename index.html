Welcome to the Cat Witch Mission


Our cat witch, as a loyal companion, needs your help in order to save its witch friend which was taken captive by the evil witch.

How to Play

  • Move the cat witch by using the up and down arrow keys.
  • Collect the potions ans the spell books for 10 points.
  • Avoid the evil spells shots from the evil witch.
  • If you hit the evil spells three times: GAME OVER!
  • If you want to play again, just simply click on the replay button.
